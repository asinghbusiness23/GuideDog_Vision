AUTOMATIC PROTECTION
App constantly scans and warns you about obstacles, stairs, walls, doors automatically. You don't need to do anything!
Live Captions (CC button)
Tap CC to turn on closed captions. Speech near you will appear as text at the bottom of the screen in real time.
Sound Detection (🔊 button)
Tap 🔊 to enable sound detection. GuideDog will identify sounds like alarms, sirens, dogs, traffic, music, and more.
Voice Commands (tap mic button)
"What's around" - describe area
"Is it safe" - check path
"Left" / "Right" - check sides
"Read this" - read text aloud
"What color" - name a color
"What bill" - identify US currency
"Stop" / "Resume" - pause alerts
Gesture Controls
Tap TOP = describe area
Tap BOTTOM = detailed scan
Swipe LEFT/RIGHT = check sides
Alert Sounds
Beeps indicate direction of obstacle
Fast beeps = DANGER, very close!
Vibration = obstacle in your path
