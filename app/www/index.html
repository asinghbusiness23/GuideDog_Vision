Privacy & Permissions
Camera & LiDAR
GuideDog needs camera access and LiDAR to detect obstacles. All detection runs on your device. Please allow camera access when prompted.
Microphone & Speech
GuideDog uses your microphone for voice commands ("what's around", "is it safe"). Speech recognition runs on-device. Please allow microphone and speech recognition when prompted.
Cloud AI (Optional)
When you scan, a photo may be sent to a cloud AI for advanced scene description. No images are stored after analysis.
No Account Required
No personal data is collected. No sign-up needed.

GuideDog
AUTOMATIC PROTECTION
GuideDog constantly monitors your surroundings using LiDAR and AI. It will automatically warn you about walls, obstacles, and hazards. You don't need to do anything — just walk.
GESTURES
Hold screen — speak a voice command
Double tap — scan surroundings
Swipe left/right — check sides
VOICE COMMANDS
"What's around" — describe area
"Is it safe" — check path
"Left" / "Right" — check sides
"Stop" / "Resume" — pause or resume alerts
"Scan" — detailed scan
DISTANCE ALERTS
Live distance is shown in feet. You'll hear "Stop" when something is very close, and haptic feedback matches the urgency.
CAMERA PREVIEW
Tap the CAM button (top right) to toggle the camera view on or off. Useful for sighted helpers to see what the app sees. Camera is off by default to save battery.
COLORBLIND MODE — off
Tap to switch alert colors to a red-green colorblind-friendly palette.
